Lasagne

Ingredients

  • Pasta sauce (500mL)
  • Bechemel (500mL)
  • Tomato paste (1 tablespoon)
  • Lasagne sheets (6)
  • Shredded cheese (100g)
  • Minced pork/beef (500g)
  • 1 diced onion
  • 4 cloves of chopped garlic
  • Salt (1 tablespoon)
  • 4 grated carrots (boiled)
  • Random herbs such as oregano or basil

Steps

  1. Fry onion and garlic in a pan with some oil (1 tablespoon)
  2. Add meat and herbs
  3. Once meat is mostly cooked, add grated carrot (boiled preferrably unless you like some crunch in your lasagne)
  4. Add tomato paste and pasta sauce
  5. Line a baking dish with half of the cooked ragu (pasta meat sauce mixture)
  6. Add 2 lasagne sheets on top
  7. Add 50g of grated cheese and half the jar of bechemel on top
  8. Add another 2 lasagne sheets then the remaining ragu sauce on top
  9. Finish with the remaining bechemel and grated cheese on top
  10. Bake in a 200 degree fan forced oven for 35 minutes. Cover the dish with baking paper to prevent the cheese burning
