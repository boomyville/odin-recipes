Fried instant Noodles

Ingredients

  • 2 packets of instant noodles
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 200g of minced meat (pork or beef)
  • 2 eggs
  • Random vegetables (such as bok choi or choi sum)

Steps

  1. Scramble 2 eggs
  2. Move eggs aside then fry the minced meat
  3. Add soy sauce and any other seasonings such as MSG. Good time to add vegetables now.
  4. On the side cook the instant noodles in boiling water for 2 minutes
  5. Drain water from noodles and add to the pan with the cooked meat
  6. Combine with fried egg into a bowl
