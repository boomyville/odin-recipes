Smoothie

Preparation

  • Nutribullet or smoothie machine
  • 200g of favourite fruit frozen (frozen mango from the supermarket works)
  • 200mL (plant based) milk (oat milk is my favourite)
  • 2 tablespoons of vanilla or flavoured yoghurt
  • 1 scoop of vanilla ice-cream (optional)
  • 1 super ripe banana (optional)

Steps

  • Chuck all ingredients into the bottle for blending
  • Blend for at least 20 seconds. Give a good shake
